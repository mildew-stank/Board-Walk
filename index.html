About

Board Walk is meant to help you learn the fretboard of a guitar, and how to play in key. Type the name of a key into the search bar and corresponding notes will be highlighted.

Supported keys

  • Major
  • Minor
  • Pentatonic Major
  • Pentatonic Minor

Supported chords

  • Major Triad
  • Minor Triad
  • Major Seventh
  • Minor Seventh
  • Power
  • Dominant Seventh
  • Diminished
  • Diminished Seventh
  • Major Diad
  • Minor Diad
  • Augmented
  • Augmented Seventh
