Board Walk

About

Board Walk is meant to help you learn the fretboard of a guitar, and how to play in key. Type the name of a key into the search bar and corresponding notes will be highlighted.

Supported keys

  • Major
  • Minor
  • Pentatonic Major
  • Pentatonic Minor
